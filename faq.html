Frequently Asked Questions

Who we are?

Get Capital is an online marketplace that connect small business owners with multiple lending products provided by our hand-picked lending partners to help businesses get access to the capital needed to grow.

Is Get Capital a lender?

No, Get Capital is not a lender. Our job is connecting you with the best financing options available for your business. We work with a network of hand-picked lending partners that offer different lending products so you can choose the once that fits your business needs.

Is Get Capital a secure platform?

Keeping your information safe is high priority for us. We use bank-level security standards to keep your files safe and secure.

What are the interest rates and when do I need to payback?

Interest rates and terms can vary widely depending on your business finances and the type of loan you are applying for. Credit history and how quickly a borrower needs money also is an important factor in determining the interest rate and repayment terms.

What are the fees for usign the platform or checking my rate?

$0, none, nada. Get Capital does not charge any fee for using our platform or checking a rate for a loan. Also checking your rate wont affect your credit score!.

There are hidden fees?

No. Our commitment is to 100% transparency to make the lending ecosystem healthier and better, so once again, using our platform or checking your rate is 100% free.

So, how Get Capital makes money?

We only make money when you succeed in securing a loan. Every time a loan is successfully funded, the lender pays us a % of the total amount as a broker fee.

We arenâ€™t rewarded for working with specific lenders over others, so you can be 100% sure that the loan you get is the best for your business.

How Get Capital select the lending partners?

Our process of selection is 100% hand-picked, so everytime we add a new lending partner to our platform, we consider many factors like: track record, average rates, transparency and customer service. We work hard to select only the best lenders that qualify for our high standards so you can get the best funding options available in the market.

How long does the process take and how fast I can get the money?

That depends on the type of loan and the lending parter you choose to work with. The process can take as little as 48 hours to four weeks. It is important to note that lenders use different underwriting processes and technologies so some of them are able to provide you with the capital faster then others.
